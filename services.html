Class 4

In Canada, the Class 4 driver's license is typically required for operating a taxi or ride-sharing vehicle. The specific requirements and regulations can vary slightly from one province or territory to another.

Class 5

In Canada, the Class 5 driver's license is the most common type of license and is typically required for operating standard passenger vehicles, including cars, vans, and small trucks. The specific requirements for obtaining a Class 5 license can vary slightly from one province or territory to another.

Class 7

In Canada, the Class 7 driver's license is commonly known as a "learner's license." It is designed for new drivers who are just starting to learn how to drive. The specific requirements and regulations for obtaining a Class 7 license can vary from one province or territory to another.
